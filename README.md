# Revenue Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing 4 years (2015–2018) of sales data to track revenue, profit, and customer performance, and to surface actionable business insights for leadership.

## Overview

This project transforms raw transactional sales data into an executive-ready dashboard covering revenue trends, profitability, regional performance, sales rep contribution, and return rates — enabling stakeholders to monitor KPIs and drill into performance drivers without needing to touch the underlying data.

## Key Features

KPI Summary Cards — Total Customers, Orders, Revenue, Quantity Sold, Profit, and Average Discount at a glance
Revenue & Profit Trend — Monthly trend line (2015–2018) highlighting seasonal peaks and troughs
Category Breakdown — Revenue and profit by product category (Technology, Office Supplies, Furniture)
Regional Performance — Revenue and profit by region (Central, North, South)
Sales Rep Performance — Revenue contribution and share by sales representative
Returns Analysis — Year-over-year return volume tracked against revenue growth
Target Tracking — Gauge visual comparing actual revenue to target sales
Interactive Slicers — Filter by City, Country, Order Year, Order Day, Segment, and Sales Rep

Clone or download this repository
Open Revenue_Analysis.pbix in Power BI Desktop
Use the slicers on the left to filter by city, country, year, day, segment, or sales rep
Hover over visuals for detailed tooltips
